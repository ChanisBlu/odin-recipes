Description

Cornflakes cereal is a quick and nutritious breakfast made from toasted corn flakes, usually served with cold milk or yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of cornflakes
  • 1 cup of cold milk
  • Sugar or honey to taste (optional)
  • Chopped fruit (banana, strawberries, apple, etc., optional)

Steps

  1. Put the cornflakes in a bowl.
  2. Pour the cold milk over them.
  3. Add sugar or honey if you like.
  4. Add chopped fruit for extra flavor and nutrition.
  5. Mix lightly and enjoy immediately.
