Description

Tortilla, meat and some vegetables (the sauce is optional)

Ingredients

(for 4 servings)

  • 8 tortillas
  • 450 grams of beef
  • 1/2 white onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil or lard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • (Optional) a splash of lime juice or white vinegar to marinate
  • (Optional) a bit of achiote, paprika, or ground guajillo chili for extra flavor and color
  • Red salsa and/or green salsa (your favorite type)

Steps

  1. Finely chop the beef into small taquería-style pieces.
  2. In a large pan, heat the oil or lard over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the beef and let it sear without stirring too much at first.
  4. Once it releases some juice, add the onion
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a splash of lime or vinegar if desired.
  6. Cook until the meat is browned and juicy (about 8–10 minutes).
  7. Warm the tortillas on a hot pan or comal until soft and fragrant.
  8. Spoon the meat onto the tortillas (use two if they’re thin).
  9. Top with onion, cilantro, and salsa of your choice.
  10. Squeeze fresh lime on top and enjoy — like at a real Mexican street taquería!
